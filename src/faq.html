FAQ

Some frequently asked questions answered for you.

How much does it cost?

Nothing. It's free :)

How can I get there?

Take a look at Location.

I have applied, but cannot attend.

Then please go to the system where you have gotten your ticket and unsubscribe from it, so others can take your place!

Where can I sleep?

The hackathon is meant as a place to work, so the people are meant to work trough the night, not sleep. Nethertheless we have a little quiet place to sleep in the zkm. If you plan to take a nap, please carry a sleeping bag or at least some sort of pillow with you.

I have never been to a Hackathon, what now?

Come over! Try it! We try to recruit as many new hackers as possible!

What is the team size?

We encourage everyone to participate in 4 person teams.

What should I bring?

All you really need for a hackathon is your laptop, charger calbe and motivatoin. If you stay overnight, bring some items for personal hygiene and a sleeping bag or some sort of pillow if your planning to take nap.

I arrive on Friday and need accomodation!

Checkout the Hostels in Karlsruhe here and here.
We are working on some special deals for our participants that travel from further away. Stay tuned!
