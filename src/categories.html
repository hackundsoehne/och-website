Our main category is completely free, you can do whatever you want.

There's only one rule: Don't start working on your project until the hackathon begins. You can research it, overthink it or even bother your already annoyed neighbours with it. Just don't start too early.

For those looking to solve more specific problems there are the three categories powered by our partners.

How can I win?

In the "Hack & Söhne" category we'll provide an elaborate evaluation process with a jury of seven professionals from various fields. Read more about the jury and the selection process in the jury section.

  • Topic

    If your are looking for clear guidelines on what to build, where to look and how to implement your idea, this is the wrong category to focus on. You can submit any idea in this category. Be it a web-app, a Haskell API or a creative piece of digital art, our jurors will decide which project is the strongest overall.

    Prizes

    The first place in our category wins 4000€, the second gets 2000€ and the third gets 1000€.

    Judging Criteria

    Each juror judges the submitted ideas according to their field of expertise. We have artists, developers, entrepreneurs and investors. If your idea can convince the majority of them, you just might win.

    Judging in the preselection phase will be done using Gavel (here's why), to ensure fair and transparent judgements.

  • Topic

    Hi, we’re CAS. We love bringing people together, worldwide. That’s an exciting and big challenge and therefore we need open-minded people, who want do be part of something big. People with dedication, creativity and skills. That’s where you come in.

    Your contribution comes from exploring new ways of connecting people through technology. Build something awesome that simplifies communication, enables collaboration, or just brings people closer together. This can be anything that you can imagine.

    The only thing that matters: Create something from which your friends, family, club or society as a whole can benefit. Happiness comes from solving problems. You decide which technology you choose to build an app, website, integrating service… You think of more than that? Feel free.

    And how do you succeed in this category? Important to us is not a completely finished, absolute perfect solution. Done is better than perfect. You can win our hearts and the prize by developing a stunning prototype, a creative concept or even a breakthrough idea. If we can support you in any way, e.g. finding the right idea, just come to our spot at ZKM during the hackathon. And now it is up to you…

  • Topic

    In a world that grows more volatile, uncertain, complex and ambiguous, integration is the key to cope with the challenges arising with every new possibility. So, we are looking for inventive ways to combine technologies, business sectors, value chains, ecosystems, data, assets or even cultures.

    Big ideas need a lot of bridging between things that don't seem to naturally belong together: in a smart city, millions of devices need to communicate not only with each other, but also bring information from the material world into the digital. To build new solutions in mobility and transport, we have to cross borders in a technical sense as well as in the user's experience. To live peacefully in a global society, we have to embrace and integrate different cultures and enable decentral communities, grids and communication.

    We want you to build the first step towards a more connected future, enabling cutting-edge use cases, or making new things possible within the old. We value passion, creativity in applying technologies, novelty and disruptive potential, but also outstanding teamwork. The winner of our challenge can look forward to hacker-approved prizes.

    To help you build the technology of tomorrow, we provide you with useful APIs and data. Come to our booth to get an introduction or help from our experts. Let's shape the future by connecting worlds!
